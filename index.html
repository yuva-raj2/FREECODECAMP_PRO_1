Cup of Coffee

Now we are going to look about the preparation of a cup of coffee

Ingredients

  • Washed Cup
  • Vessels
  • Filter
  • A little quantity of Hot Water
  • Milk
  • Coffee Powder
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. We need a cup and vessels and it should be washed.
  2. Take a little cup of water.
  3. Pour a little cup of water in the milk and add a bit of sugar as much you want but for one person minimum 2 spoons is enough.
  4. Stir it well till the sugar grinds.
  5. Boil the milk in the induction stove.
  6. After boiling pour it in the cup and it should be filtered using filter material.
  7. Now you can mix the coffee powder with milk.
  8. Stir it well till the coffee powder grinds.
